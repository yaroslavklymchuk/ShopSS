ABOUT US

Our purpose is to protect nature and make art.

ABSTRACT FLOW

Our first collection inspired by artists who create pieces of art in a new field of art that came to be known as Abstract Expressionism.

​

We curated works of Felicity Nove, Jay Le Feo and Jet Le Parti.

Felicity Nove

An artist from Los Angeles, USA. 
Felicity Nove's paintings capture gestural anomalies that stretch moments into eternity. Her paintings burst and bubble, placid lakes smooth over existing formations, or crags and crevices are left high and dry, leaving us to ponder what sort of natural and unnatural mechanisms have led to their creation and how we might be affected as well.

Website

Jay DeFeo 

Mary Joan Jay DeFeo (March 31, 1929 – November 11, 1989) was a visual artist associated with the Beat Generation.


Jay DeFeo began this monumental work simply as an “idea that had a center to it”.

DeFeo resisted offering an explanation or interpretation of the work, although she did acknowledge that despite the work’s enormous size and rough surfaces, there was a connection to “the way actual rose petals are formed and how they relate to each other in the flower.”

Website

Jet Le Parti

Jet Le Parti is an artist who takes the expressionist art style to a whole new level. You can enjoy his work due to its large scale and deep layering. 
He isn’t afraid to go over something he’s already made to emphasize his ideas into a deeper image.
